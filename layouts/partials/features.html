Office View 🛡️

Have access to a birds-eye view of the virtual office layout, including which room of the office each employee is in.

Customizable Avatars 🚀

Customize your avatar's clothes and facial features, so that the virtual office environment can feel more personalized.

Work Status Displayed📈

Each person in the virtual office has a status icon that depicts their availability. Toggling between "do not disturb", "on a call", and "open" allows employees to balance collaboration with the need for focused work time.

Intuitive UI🦉

Simple user interface that lets users navigate between office rooms with the press of just one button.

Personalized Offices✨

Employee's have the ability to customize their virtual office space with personal items and decor, creating a comfortable workspace that reflects each employee's personality and work style.

Break Room🧰

The virtual office offers an interactive break room with games and activities allowing employees to socialize and unwind together during breaks, fostering team bonding and reducing feelings of isolation.
